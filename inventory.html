Bisheng Du 杜碧升, PhD, Associate Professor

Department of Management Science & Engineering

Homepage www.dubisheng.com

库存控制，英语是Inventory Control，是供应链管理的重要组成部分。库存的模型分为静态模型和动态模型。

有几本经典的著作是学习库存控制模型的基础。在库存控制中，随机模型是重要的理论基础。

参考文献

  • Sven Axsäter. Inventory Control (3rd Edition). (2015). Inventory Control. Springer.

  • David Simchi-Levi, Xin Chen, Julien Bramel. The Logic of Logistics: Theory, Algorithms, and Applications for Logistics Management (3rd Edition). (2014). Springer.

  • Alain Bensoussan. Dynamic Programming and Inventory Control. (2011). IOS Press.

  • Donald Waters. Inventory Control and Management. (2007). John Wiley & Sons.

  • Evan L. Porteus. Foundations of Stochastic Inventory Theory. (2002). Stanford University Press.

  • Paul H. Zipkin. Foundations of Inventory Management. (2000). McGraw-Hill/Irwin.
    [美] 保罗·齐普金（Paul H.Zipkin）著 马常松 译.库存管理基础。中国财政经济出版社. 2013年06月

  • Edward A. Silver, David F. Pyke, Rein Peterson. Inventory Management and Production Planning and Scheduling. (1998). Wiley.

  • George F. Hadley, Thomson M. Whitin. Analysis of Inventory Systems. (1963). Prentice-Hall, Englewood Cliffs.
